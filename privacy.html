Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

1. No Personal Data Collection

AniMatch does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any identifying details. There are no accounts, no logins, and no external tracking analytics.

2. Local Data Storage Only

All of your data — including your Watchlist, Liked/Skipped anime, Statistics, Taste Profile, and Achievements — is stored locally on your device using browser-standard standard Web Storage (localStorage and IndexedDB). If you uninstall the app or clear your browser data, your AniMatch data will be permanently deleted.

3. API Usage & Attribution

AniMatch requires an internet connection to fetch anime data (titles, cover images, descriptions, and scores). This data is provided by the Jikan API, an unofficial, open-source REST API for MyAnimeList.net.

When you use the app to browse anime, standard network requests are sent to Jikan's servers. We do not send your personal swipe history or preferences to Jikan; all recommendation algorithms and preference filtering happen locally on your device.

4. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixels, or any third-party advertising trackers. Your usage of the app remains entirely private.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Because we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact the developer through the app's official channels or via the Google Play Store listing.
